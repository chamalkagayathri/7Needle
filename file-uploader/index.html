Fonterra Flare Program: Video Submission Guidelines

Duration: Keep the video exactly 1 minute long.

Content: Include a brief introduction, your passion for the dairy industry, key skills, and your vision for joining the Flare Program.

Format: Submit in MP4 or MOV format, with a resolution of at least 720p and a file size no larger than 100MB.

Creativity: Feel free to use creative elements, but keep the focus on your message
